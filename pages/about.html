Our Mission

We believe file conversion should be simple, private, and accessible. No accounts, no uploads, no subscriptions — just powerful tools that work right in your browser.

How It Works

Every conversion happens locally on your device using modern web technologies like WebAssembly and Web Workers. We use FFmpeg.wasm for video, PDF.js for documents, and browser-native APIs for images and audio. Nothing is ever sent to a server.

Why Choose Us

Privacy First. Your files contain sensitive information — personal photos, business documents, private recordings. They should never leave your device, and with Fluxora, they never do.

One-Time Pricing. We don't believe in charging you every month. Pay once, and the tools are yours forever — including future updates.

Global Performance. Served through Cloudflare's global CDN, the site loads fast wherever you are. The heavy lifting happens on your device, not our servers.

Technology

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no bloat. Three.js powers our dynamic backgrounds. Cloudflare Workers handle license verification at the edge.
