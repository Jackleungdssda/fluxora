10 Best Free Online File Converters in 2026

Whether you need to convert a PDF to JPG, compress a video for social media, or batch-process images, online file converters have become essential tools. But not all converters are equal — some compromise your privacy, others deliver poor output quality, and many hide premium features behind aggressive paywalls. We tested 25+ services to bring you the definitive ranking for 2026.

How We Evaluated

Each converter was scored across four dimensions: privacy (does it upload your files?), conversion quality (output fidelity), speed (processing time), and value (free tier vs paid). Services that process files locally in the browser received a privacy bonus. Cloud-based services were penalized if their privacy policy allowed indefinite file retention or data sharing with third parties.

#1 Fluxora — Best Overall
All processing happens in your browser via WebAssembly and Canvas APIs. Zero file uploads, no registration, instant conversions for images, PDFs, audio, and video. Free tier with HD downloads available via affordable lifetime plans ($0.99–$3.99). The only converter that combines privacy, quality, and value without compromise.
#2 CloudConvert — Widest Format Support
Supports 200+ formats with a polished cloud-based pipeline. Free tier limited to 25 conversions per day. Quality is excellent but files are uploaded to their servers and stored for up to 24 hours. Best for rare formats and bulk API automation.
#3 Zamzar — Best for Email Conversion
Venerable service established in 2006. Supports file conversion via email attachment — send a file, get the result back. Free tier capped at 2 files per day (50MB max). Good for occasional one-off conversions but the upload model raises privacy concerns for sensitive documents.
#4 Convertio — Best UI, Cloud-Based
Sleek interface with drag-and-drop workflow. Supports OCR for scanned documents. Free tier: 10 conversions per day. Files are uploaded to their servers. Premium plans from $9.99/month. Good choice if you need OCR capabilities.
#5 FreeConvert — Good All-Rounder
Clean interface with advanced options for compression and resizing. Free tier allows files up to 1GB — generous compared to competitors. Cloud-based with a clear deletion policy (files removed after 4 hours). Good for large video files.
#6 ILovePDF — Best for PDF-Only Workflows
Dedicated PDF toolkit: merge, split, compress, convert to/from Office formats. Free tier processes files up to 100MB. Clean, fast interface. Files are uploaded to their servers. Premium at $7/month unlocks batch processing and unlimited size.
#7 Online-Convert — Most Configuration Options
Deep customization: bitrate, resolution, codec selection, metadata editing. Good for technical users who need precise control. Cloud-based with reasonable privacy policy. Free tier limited to 100MB per file.
#8 AVC Labs — Best for Audio
Specializes in audio format conversion with excellent codec support including FLAC, ALAC, and DSD. Free desktop app beats cloud alternatives for audio quality. Windows/Mac only — not browser-based.
#9 Adobe Acrobat Web — Enterprise PDF Solution
Industry-standard PDF engine with the best fidelity for complex layouts. Free tier limited to 2 tasks per month. Best quality but expensive for regular use ($19.99/month). Overkill for casual conversion needs.
#10 HandBrake — Best Desktop Video Transcoder
Open-source video transcoder with professional-grade encoding options. Not browser-based but free and powerful. Best for users comfortable with desktop software who need granular control over video output.

The Privacy Factor: Browser vs Cloud

The most important trend in 2026 is the shift toward browser-based processing. Services like Fluxora leverage WebAssembly to run conversion engines entirely on your device — your files never leave your browser. This eliminates the risk of data breaches, unauthorized retention, and third-party access that plague cloud-based services. For tax documents, contracts, personal photos, and business files, browser-based conversion is objectively safer.

Bottom Line

For everyday conversions where privacy matters, Fluxora is the clear winner — no uploads, instant results, and affordable lifetime access. For edge cases requiring ultra-rare formats or OCR, CloudConvert and Convertio are solid cloud-based alternatives. Just remember: if a service is free and cloud-based, your files may be the product.
